Legendary Musicians

Discover the lives and achievements of world-famous musicians whose talent and creativity have inspired millions across generations.

Michael Jackson

Born: 29 August 1958

Died: 25 June 2009

Nationality: American

Profession: Singer, Songwriter & Dancer

Michael Jackson, known as the "King of Pop," was one of the most influential entertainers in history. His music, dance performances, and innovative music videos transformed the global music industry.

Major Achievements

  • Over 400 Million Records Sold Worldwide
  • 13 Grammy Awards
  • Rock and Roll Hall of Fame Inductee
  • One of the Best-Selling Music Artists of All Time

A. R. Rahman

Born: 6 January 1967

Nationality: Indian

Profession: Music Composer & Singer

A. R. Rahman is one of India's most celebrated music composers. His work in Indian and international cinema has earned worldwide recognition for its originality and excellence.

Major Achievements

  • Academy Award (Oscar) Winner
  • Two Grammy Awards
  • Padma Bhushan Recipient
  • Composer of Slumdog Millionaire Soundtrack
