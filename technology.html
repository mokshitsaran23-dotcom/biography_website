Technology Leaders

Discover the innovators and visionaries who transformed the world through groundbreaking technology, software, and innovation.

Steve Jobs

Born: 24 February 1955

Nationality: American

Profession: Entrepreneur & Inventor

Steve Jobs was the co-founder of Apple Inc. and one of the most influential innovators in modern technology. He revolutionized personal computing, smartphones, music players, and digital animation.

Major Achievements

  • Co-founded Apple Inc.
  • Introduced the iPhone and iPad
  • Transformed the personal computer industry
  • Former CEO of Pixar Animation Studios

Bill Gates

Born: 28 October 1955

Nationality: American

Profession: Entrepreneur & Philanthropist

Bill Gates is the co-founder of Microsoft Corporation. He played a major role in making personal computers accessible worldwide and is now recognized for his global philanthropic work.

Major Achievements

  • Co-founded Microsoft
  • Popularized Windows Operating System
  • Founded the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation
  • Global Leader in Philanthropy
