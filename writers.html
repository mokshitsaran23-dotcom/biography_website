Famous Writers

Explore the lives and literary achievements of renowned writers whose works have inspired generations around the world.

William Shakespeare

Born: April 1564

Died: 23 April 1616

Nationality: English

Profession: Playwright & Poet

William Shakespeare is regarded as one of the greatest writers in the English language. His plays and poems have influenced literature, theatre, and culture for more than four centuries.

Major Achievements

  • Wrote 39 Plays
  • Authored 154 Sonnets
  • Known as England's National Poet
  • Works translated into over 100 languages

J. K. Rowling

Born: 31 July 1965

Nationality: British

Profession: Author & Philanthropist

J. K. Rowling is the creator of the Harry Potter series, one of the best-selling book franchises in history. Her books have inspired millions of readers worldwide.

Major Achievements

  • Author of the Harry Potter Series
  • Over 600 Million Books Sold
  • Multiple British Book Awards
  • Founder of Several Charitable Organizations
