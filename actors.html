Actors

Explore the lives, achievements, and inspiring journeys of legendary actors who have shaped the world of cinema.

Leonardo DiCaprio

Born: 11 November 1974

Nationality: American

Occupation: Actor & Film Producer

Leonardo DiCaprio is one of Hollywood's most celebrated actors, known for his powerful performances in films such as Titanic, Inception, The Revenant, and The Wolf of Wall Street.

Achievements

  • Academy Award for Best Actor
  • Golden Globe Awards Winner
  • Environmental Activist

Tom Hanks

Born: 9 July 1956

Nationality: American

Occupation: Actor & Filmmaker

Tom Hanks is one of the most respected actors in cinema, famous for Forrest Gump, Cast Away, Saving Private Ryan, Apollo 13, and the Toy Story series.

Achievements

  • Two Academy Awards
  • Presidential Medal of Freedom
  • Four Golden Globe Awards
